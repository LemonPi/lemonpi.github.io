Hi, my name is Johnson, the son of my father, John. My Chinese name is Zhong Sheng (钟盛) which is pronounced similarly.

I received my PhD in Robotics advised by Dmitry Berenson and co-advised by Nima Fazeli at the University of Michigan. I previously received my B.ASc. in Engineering Science from the University of Toronto.

I am interested in using model-based methods to reason about uncertainty for robot manipulation, with the goal of enabling robots in homes. I also develop open source software for robotics research, focusing on parallelization and performance.
